About

// the real story

My marketing education didn't come from a course — it came from the cash counter of a hardware shop in Riyadh.

I came from Bangladesh on a worker visa. I run the shop floor from morning to late night — customers, suppliers, deliveries. In the gaps between customers, I taught myself SEO, Google Ads, and code. Then I built the website for the shop I work at, ranked it on Google, and ran the ad campaigns that make its phone ring.

No desk. No team. No spare time. Just proof. I know where every riyal of a business comes from, because I stand at the counter where it's earned. That's the "builds" in the name — and it's how I treat every project I touch.
